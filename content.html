ASA Datafest Presentation on Coworking Spaces

The American Statistical Association's Datafest is a 3-day data analysis competition for undergraduate students to showcase their technical & soft skills. In 2025, one of the events was held at RIT, my university; my team, The Horrors, LLC, got 3rd in the "Best Insights" category (despite being our first year participating!).

The dataset was comprised of leasing and property information for a leasing company focused in the corporate leasing sector. Out of the various industries in the dataset, we decided to focus on determining trends in successful Coworking companies, creating a model for the properties they leased that are factors of their success.
